About

Hi, I'm GEB. I'm an 18 year old singer songwriter from Bedfordshire, England, and I'm trying to build a following so that I can share my music with more and more people. Music has always been important to me, especially live music, since I was a very young age. Some of my fondest, earliest memories are of my family going to see my dad play his local gigs, and his charisma on stage and the professionalism that he performed with made a lasting impact on me.

Today, I write my own music and play some covers, and I'm always looking for an opportunity to play to an audience, so if you are looking for a high quality, professional, engaging and local act to book, don't hesitate to get in touch!
