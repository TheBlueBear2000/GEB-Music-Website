About

I am GEB, a local musician and singer/songwriter. I specialize in acoustic guitar and have a passion for creating and performing original music.

Music has been a part of my life for as long as I can remember. Growing up, I was always singing and playing an instrument. As I got older, I began writing my own songs and performing at local venues.

My music is inspired by a variety of genres, from folk and blues to rock and pop. I strive to create music that is both meaningful and enjoyable to listen to.
